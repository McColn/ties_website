TIES Overview

Tanzania Integrated Engineering Solution Company Ltd (TIES) is a private company limited by shares and is wholly owned by Tanzanians. TIES was established on April 18th, 2019 (Certificate of Incorporation No. 139070410) under companies Act, 2002; under the laws of The United Republic of Tanzania. It is also registered with Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) with Tax payer Identification number (TIN), 139-070-410.

The core business of the company is CONSULTANCY SERVICES in all engineering related activities (i.e., Geology, Mining, Mineral Processing, Environment, Electrical, Civil, Telecom,Mechanical, etc).

TIES employs high calibre Senior staff and use her local and international affiliations to support our team in providing unique solutions to clients anywhere in the region.
